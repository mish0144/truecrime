I Am a Killer

I Am a Killer is a series on Netflix and Crime+Investigation featuring interviews with death row inmates.[1][2] Season 2 aired in the United Kingdom in 2019 and aired internationally on Netflix from January 31, 2020.[3] On August 30, 2022, Season 3 aired on Netflix in the United States.[4] A fourth season was released on December 21, 2022, on Netflix.
